BAIDOO SAMUEL ABOTAR

Measurement of time

Approx. 366 days is the time taken by earth to rotate once around sun.
This is called a year.
The time taken by earth to rotateon its axis once is 24 hours.
This is called a day.

We fly to thy patronage.

We fly to thy patronage,
o holy Mother of God;
despise not our petitions in our necessities,
but deiver us always from all dangers,
o glourious and blessed virgin.
Amen.
